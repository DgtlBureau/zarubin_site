## About

The field service management (FSM) and field operations (FieldOps) sectors are undergoing significant transformations, driven by technological advancements, evolving workforce dynamics, and the increasing complexity of infrastructure. PersoniWay is an AI-first platform designed specifically for field service environments — using a "drawings-first" approach to tackle the industry's biggest problems head-on.

## The FSM Market Context

The FSM market is growing fast. According to MarketsandMarkets, the global FSM market is projected to grow from USD 4.0 billion in 2023 to USD 7.3 billion by 2028, at a CAGR of 12.8%. This growth is fueled by increasing demand for efficient field operations across HVAC, utilities, and construction.

In the United States alone, there are approximately 5.9 million commercial buildings encompassing 97 billion square feet of floor space. As commercial infrastructure grows, so does the demand for streamlined maintenance, HVAC services, and efficient facility operations.

## Problem

**Workforce pressure.** The U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics projects about 42,500 job openings annually for HVAC mechanics and installers. Add electricians, plumbers, and facility techs — and you have a workforce pipeline under pressure. Young professionals entering the trades bring new values: digital fluency, work-life balance, and a preference for tools that reduce friction in the field. Companies that fail to provide modern tools will struggle to retain talent.

**Fragmented processes.** FieldOps businesses run a web of critical processes — scheduling and dispatch, ticketing, inventory, reporting, compliance. Many rely on legacy systems or disconnected spreadsheets. Fragmentation creates delays, miscommunication, and increased operational costs.

**Customer journey failures.** Every service business has a customer journey — whether implicit or intentional. From request submission to job resolution, customers expect speed, transparency, and professionalism. Failures in communication, knowledge gaps in the field, and missing documentation all reduce NPS and profitability.

## Scope of Work

**AI-Powered Ticketing** — Automates issue classification and routes tasks instantly.

**Drawing Recognition** — Parses and indexes HVAC schematics for immediate access in the field. The AI reads, indexes, and serves schematics in real-time based on what the technician is asking — "Which controller handles this VAV?" or "Where's the RS-485 termination?" No more flipping PDFs or waiting for a callback from engineering.

**Secure Documentation** — Keeps all diagrams, specs, and revisions versioned and searchable.

**Voice Tools** — Supports audio-based queries and real-time team calls — recorded and summarized automatically.

**Insights & Reporting** — Tracks resolution time, education gaps, technician engagement, and more.

These tools don't replace field workers — they empower them. One engineer can now support 10–15 technicians remotely, guiding cases using structured knowledge and real-time interaction.

## Implementation Notes

Field service companies often ask: "Will this work with our existing workflows?" "What if we don't have clean documentation?" "Will our techs adopt it or ignore it?" PersoniWay is built with those questions in mind:

- Integrates smoothly with common CRMs and scheduling systems.
- Builds knowledge even from fragmented data — learning from past tickets and audio summaries.
- Designed for technicians with mobile-first UX and voice interaction that requires zero typing.

Rather than replacing existing systems, it enhances them.

## Result

A regional HVAC contractor managing 80 buildings with just 4 engineers and 30 junior technicians deployed PersoniWay. Prior to automation, routine troubleshooting could take hours — phone chains, misplaced schematics, guesswork. After deployment, engineers began resolving 60% of incoming tickets through remote triage. One technician noted: *"I stopped calling in for diagrams — I just speak into the app and get what I need."* That shift alone saved over 40 engineering hours per month.
