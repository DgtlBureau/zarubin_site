## About

PersoniWay is an AI assistant designed to transform the way HVAC specialists and building management professionals handle routine tasks. It accelerates problem-solving, enhances operational efficiency, and minimizes costly errors — essentially a purpose-built ChatGPT for HVAC on-site technicians.

It was a freezing Monday morning when Mike, a field technician with over a decade of experience, received an urgent call. A **Distech ECB-650** controller at a commercial building in downtown Chicago had stopped responding. In the past, this job would have meant hours of troubleshooting, flipping through manuals, calling a senior engineer for guidance, and manually tracing every connection. With PersoniWay, he typed a quick question — *"Error code 17 on Distech ECB-650. What's the fix?"* — and within seconds the system retrieved relevant technical documentation, real-world troubleshooting examples, and an annotated diagram of the ECB-650's wiring configuration.

Within ten minutes, Mike had located a slightly loose RS-485 cable, secured the connection, and reset the controller. A job that once took hours was resolved in minutes.

## Problem

For HVAC technicians, speed and accuracy are everything. Every minute spent searching for information is a minute lost in productivity. Traditional troubleshooting requires years of hands-on experience, expensive training programs, and direct access to senior engineers who may not always be available.

One of the biggest challenges in the industry is the varying skill levels of technicians. Not every field tech has the experience of a senior engineer, but customers expect every job to be completed with the same level of expertise. Companies face labor shortages, rising costs, and inconsistent first-time fix rates.

## Scope of Work

**AI Chat Assistant** trained on technical documentation, diagrams, and previous tickets. It helps technicians on-site:

- Pinpoint the right controller instantly, identifying its role and function.
- Map system connections, showing how components interact for seamless diagnostics.
- Consolidate all technical data into an accessible, structured knowledge base — available 24/7.
- Retrieve wiring diagrams, manuals, and part numbers instantly.
- Work via voice commands, integrating seamlessly with field workflows.

**Ticketing System** enables dispatchers and support teams to monitor technician requests in real-time, assign specialists or escalate to supervisors, attach internal notes and files, and analyze incident types and technician performance across regions.

**Admin Dashboard** gives managers operational insights: upload and manage the knowledge base, configure AI prompts and data sources, track resolution times and team workloads, and export analytics for quality assurance.

## How the AI Layer Works

1. **Data Processing & Vectorization** — AI ingests manufacturer documentation, service logs, and past troubleshooting cases, converting them into searchable vector embeddings. Queries are compared against thousands of past cases to retrieve the most relevant solutions in real-time.

2. **Technical Drawings & Schematic Recognition** — The system identifies the correct schematic, then highlights the specific wiring, component, or control logic relevant to the technician's issue. When Mike searched for "Error 17 on Distech ECB-650," the AI cross-referenced known system faults and retrieved a BACnet wiring diagram with highlighted communication pathways.

3. **Real-Time Sensor & System Diagnostics** — Connects with live controller logs, analyzing BACnet data, Modbus registers, and IoT telemetry. If a sensor reading is off, the AI can predict the root cause, reducing unnecessary component replacements.

4. **Automated Knowledge Transfer** — Learns from past fixes, ensuring expert knowledge remains available to all technicians, including those with less field experience. Reduces dependency on direct mentorship from senior engineers.

The platform is built on retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) architecture with vector databases for fast, contextual query of technical information. It supports mobile-first use, voice-to-text input, and multi-device syncing.
